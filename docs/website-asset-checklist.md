# Pravnya.com Website Asset Checklist

Date: 2026-07-01
Owner: Pravnix Labs
Source: Website Design & Content Handbook

## Summary

This checklist maps every required production asset for replacing current website placeholders. Do not use random
stock imagery. Assets should follow the handbook: warm natural light, parent-first, privacy-conscious, premium,
calm, and non-clinical.

## YouTube Demo Embed Requirements

| Filename | Dimensions | Format | Page | Purpose | Priority | Replacement Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `demo-thumbnail-home.webp` | 1280x720 | WebP | Home or How It Works | Optional product demo preview | P2 | Use app UI and warm family context, not generic video thumbnail. |
| `demo-thumbnail-ai.webp` | 1280x720 | WebP | Ask Pravnya AI | Optional AI walkthrough preview | P2 | Show evidence-traced AI, not chatbot prompt. |

Embed requirements:

- Use privacy-enhanced YouTube domain or approved video provider.
- Lazy-load iframe only after user interaction.
- Provide transcript link.
- Provide accessible title and captions.
- Track `View Demo` event only after analytics approval.
