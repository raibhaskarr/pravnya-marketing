# 2. Why Pravnya Exists

Every parent already does this: you take the photo. You save the voice note your child left you. You text a grandparent about the new word your toddler said this morning. You have a folder — somewhere — of "moments I want to remember."

The trouble is, none of it lives in one place.

A milestone gets captured on a phone that later runs out of storage. A worry you meant to mention to the paediatrician gets lost between one appointment and the next. A pattern that would be obvious if you saw six months of notes side by side stays invisible, because those notes are scattered across three apps, a notes file, and your memory.

And when it's time to share — with a partner, a grandparent, a teacher, a therapist — you're often reconstructing everything from scratch, from memory, the night before it matters most.

Pravnya was built to close that gap: to bring the moments, the memories, and the meaning into one trusted place — organised automatically, easy to search, and ready whenever you need it.

> **In short:** Parents already collect the memories. Pravnya helps you keep them, understand them, and use them.

---

# 3. What is Pravnya?

Pravnya brings together everything a family needs to understand a child's developmental story, in one app:

- A **development journal** — for the notes, questions, and observations you want to remember
- A **growth tracker** — that shows progress in your child's own story, not against other children
- A **family memory book** — for the photos, videos, and voice notes worth keeping
- An **AI assistant** — that helps you notice patterns in what you've already recorded
- **Progress reports** — ready to bring to any appointment or meeting
- **Secure sharing** — so you decide exactly what a professional or family member sees

**One app. One timeline. One place for your child's journey.**

> **What Pravnya is not:** Pravnya is not a medical tool, and it does not diagnose, assess, or evaluate your child. It's a companion for what you're already noticing — built to help you keep track of it, understand it, and talk about it with confidence.

# 4. Core Features

### Child Profile
Every child has their own space: basic details, the developmental areas you're keeping an eye on, and the professionals involved in their care. You build it gradually — nothing has to be filled in on day one.
**Benefit:** Everything about your child's journey starts from one organised place.

### Today
Your home screen. It shows what you've recently captured, any AI insight that's ready for you, and puts the capture button one tap away.
**Benefit:** A quick daily check-in — not another dashboard to study.

### Quick Capture
The fastest way to add anything to your child's record — in whichever form fits the moment.
**Benefit:** A thirty-second voice note captured in the moment beats a perfectly written paragraph that never gets written.
**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER — Quick Capture options]**

### Photos
Capture a moment visually, straight from your camera or photo library, with a short note explaining what it shows.
**Benefit:** Turns a photo into a searchable, meaningful part of your child's record — not just another file in your camera roll.

### Videos
Add a short video when something is better shown than described — a new movement, a first attempt at a word, a moment of play.
**Benefit:** Captures nuance that a written note can't.

### Voice Notes
Speak instead of type. Voice notes are automatically transcribed and become fully searchable, and they work even without an internet connection — transcription completes as soon as you're back online.
**Benefit:** The lowest-effort way to capture something before it slips your mind.

### Journey
A timeline that shows your child's development as a story across time, not as a list of disconnected entries — organised by developmental domain, with milestones and professional visits marked along the way.
**Benefit:** Patterns and progress become visible in a way that's only possible when you look at weeks and months together. It's normal for this to feel sparse in the first couple of weeks — it becomes genuinely rich after a few months of steady capturing.
**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER — Journey timeline]**

### Growth
Shows movement in your child's own story — and only that. Pravnya never shows a score, a percentile, or a "behind/on track" label. You choose the time period you want to look at.
**Benefit:** Progress you can see and feel proud of, measured the only way that actually helps: against your own child, over time.
**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER — Growth view]**

### Ask Pravnya
A conversational AI that helps you understand your own observations more deeply — by reading only your child's record, never the open internet or other families' data. Every answer shows what it's based on and how confident it is, including when the evidence is limited.
**Benefit:** A thoughtful second look at what you've already noticed, without ever telling you what to do.
**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER — Ask Pravnya conversation]**

### Activities
Your complete record — every note, photo, voice memo, and document you've ever captured, newest first, and fully searchable.
**Benefit:** Go back, add a tag you forgot, or find the one entry from six months ago that suddenly matters again.

### Reports
Generates a structured summary of what your record shows — observations, what's changed, and questions worth raising — ready in minutes, before any appointment.
**Benefit:** Many families tell us this is the single feature that makes Pravnya worth it. You walk in prepared instead of trying to remember everything on the spot.
**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER — Appointment preparation report]**

### Sharing
Two ways to share, for two different needs. For a single appointment or update, send a secure, time-limited link or an exported PDF — you choose exactly what's included. For an ongoing relationship — a co-parent, a grandparent, a regular caregiver, or a professional who's part of the team long-term — add them to "Who Has Access" on your child's profile, with their own login and exactly the permissions you choose. Either way, you can change or revoke access at any time.
**Benefit:** Whether it's a one-time update or someone who's part of your child's everyday story, nothing is shared unless you choose to share it — and you're always in control of how much.

### Help Centre
A searchable library of plain-English guidance covering every part of Pravnya, plus contextual help right where you need it on any screen.
**Benefit:** You're never more than a tap away from a clear answer.

### Privacy & Security
Your child's record is encrypted, private by default, and visible only to you unless you choose to share it. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time.
**Benefit:** Peace of mind that comes from real control, not just a promise. See Section 7 for our full commitments.

# 6. Built Around Responsible AI

We built Pravnya's AI around one belief: **AI should amplify a parent's understanding — never replace it.**

That shows up in a few concrete ways:

- **AI supports parents. It never replaces therapists, teachers, or healthcare professionals.** Pravnya helps you prepare for those conversations — it never has the last word in them.
- **Every answer is grounded in your child's own information**, and in guidance we trust — never guesswork, and never other families' data.
- **When the evidence is limited, Pravnya says so.** We would rather show honest uncertainty than a confident-sounding answer that isn't backed by what you've actually recorded.
- **You remain in control.** Every AI insight can be reviewed, corrected, or dismissed. Nothing Pravnya's AI produces becomes part of your child's record without your say.

> **A permanent commitment, not a feature:** These aren't settings that might change with the next product update. They're commitments we've published and hold ourselves to — because the moment AI becomes the authority on a child's development, rather than a support to the parent who knows that child best, the product has failed.

# 7. Privacy First

Your child's developmental record is one of the most personal things a family can keep. We treat it that way.

- **You own your data.** It belongs to your family — not to us, and not for sale.
- **Sharing is always your choice.** Nothing leaves your account unless you actively share it, and you can revoke access at any time.
- **Your data is protected.** Information is encrypted, both in transit and at rest, and private by default.
- **We will never sell your data**, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything beyond helping you and the people you choose to include.
- **Your child's record is never used to train AI models.** Improvements to Pravnya's AI come from general research — never from your family's private information.
- **You can export or permanently delete your data whenever you choose.**

> **Our promise:** We will never treat your family's trust as a commercial asset.

# 11. Frequently Asked Questions

**1. What is Pravnya, exactly?**
Pravnya is an app that helps parents capture, organise, and understand their child's developmental journey — combining a journal, a growth tracker, a memory book, an AI assistant, and secure sharing in one place.

**2. Who is Pravnya for?**
Any parent or caregiver who wants to keep a thoughtful record of their child's development — whether that's a typically developing child, a child with a developmental delay, autism, speech or language differences, or a child in early intervention.

**3. Does my child need a diagnosis to use Pravnya?**
No. Pravnya is useful to any family paying attention to their child's development, with or without a diagnosis.

**4. Does Pravnya diagnose or assess my child?**
No. Pravnya does not assess, evaluate, or diagnose. It helps you understand your own observations — it doesn't interpret them clinically.

**5. Can Pravnya give medical advice?**
No. Pravnya is not a medical app and doesn't offer medical advice. For anything medical, please speak with a qualified professional — Pravnya can help you prepare for that conversation, but it doesn't replace it.

**6. What devices does Pravnya work on?**
Pravnya is available on iPhone, Android, and the web.

**7. Can I track more than one child?**
Yes. Pravnya supports multiple children within a family.

**8. Does Pravnya work offline?**
Text and voice notes can be captured offline; voice notes are transcribed automatically once you're back online.

**9. What languages does Pravnya support?**
Pravnya currently supports English, with additional languages planned.

**10. Can my partner or co-parent see our child's record too?**
Yes. From your child's profile, add them under "Who Has Access" with a Family relationship — they'll get their own login and exactly the access you choose, from following along to adding their own observations.

**11. Is my family's data sold to anyone?**
No, never. We do not sell family data, and we never will.

**12. Is my data used to train AI models?**
No. Your child's record is never used to train AI — for your family or any other.

**13. Is my data encrypted?**
Yes. Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and it's private by default.

**14. Can I export or delete my data?**
Yes, at any time. You can request a full export of your data, or request permanent deletion.

**15. Is there a free plan?**
Yes. Pravnya offers a free plan so every family can start capturing right away, with an optional Premium plan for deeper insights and reporting. Current plan details are always available at pravnya.com.
